# Main Persona

---

## Project Manager
> "I need a one-pager that answers: are we on track, on budget, and ready to close the phase?"

### Goal
Ensure project milestones are met on-time and within budget through proactive risk management.

### KPIs
- Milestone On-Time
- Target Cost Gap
- Phase Pass Rate
- Reduce Decision Lead Time

### Pain Points & Needs

| Pain Point | Need |
|---|---|
| Fragmented Visibility — Fragmented info hides phase status | One Truth Dashboard — Phase status dashboard for instant risk visibility |
| Lagging Risk Detection — Late inter-departmental risk sharing | Proactive Escalation Triggers — Real-time alerts for schedule/cost deviations |
| Inefficient Cost-Design Loop — Endless design-RFQ loops → overruns/delays | Cost & Schedule Transparency — Design change impact tracking (BOM + timeline) |
| Unclear Supplier Feasibility — Post-contract supplier risks | Early Supplier Feasibility Check — Pre-sourcing supplier feasibility/risk review |

---

## Design Engineer
> "I want to know the cost, supplier, and feasibility the moment I change a design."

### Goal
Stabilize design maturity early to minimize late-stage engineering changes.

### KPIs
- Release On-time Rate
- Design Readiness
- Technical Issue Resolution Rate
- Engineering Change Lead Time

### Pain Points & Needs

| Pain Point | Need |
|---|---|
| Manual Rework Loops — Repetitive design-sourcing rework loops | Unified Impact View — Integrated view for instant cost/sourcing/quality info |
| Lagging Cost Visibility — No instant cost impact after design changes | Feasibility Signals — Real-time supplier manufacturing feasibility in design phase |
| Siloed Impact Analysis — Separate checks for cost/supplier/schedule impacts | Pre-freeze Risk Simulation — Pre-freeze change risk simulation |
| Late Feasibility Detection — Late supplier manufacturing constraints discovery | Traceable Change History — Design revision history and communication tracking |

---

## Cost Manager
> "I want to collaborate early to provide cost insights and use granular data to negotiate based on facts."

### Goal
Secure target cost achievement through early collaboration and data-driven insights.

### KPIs
- Target Cost Achievement Rate
- Should-Cost Accuracy Rate
- Cost Saving Contribution
- RFQ Turnaround Time

### Pain Points & Needs

| Pain Point | Need |
|---|---|
| Late-stage Involvement — Late cost reviews after design freeze limit improvements | Early Cost Integration — Proactive cost analysis from early design stages before freeze |
| Fragmented Cost Data — Scattered target costs/BOM/quotes hinder data access | Unified Cost Platform — Centralized BOM/quote data for accurate decision-making |
| Reactive Visibility — Reactive management only after budget overruns | Proactive Risk Monitoring — Real-time target cost deviation monitoring and response |
| Weak Negotiation Leverage — Weak should-cost basis undermines supplier negotiations | Benchmarked Negotiation — Transparent should-cost vs. actual quote comparison for negotiation advantage |

---

## Sourcing Manager
> "I need clear design, cost, and supplier data to select the right supplier and negotiate with confidence."

### Goal
Select the most competitive supplier by validating feasibility before RFQ award.

### KPIs
- Sourcing Cycle Time
- TCO Improvement
- Cost Saving Contribution
- RFQ Completion Time

### Pain Points & Needs

| Pain Point | Need |
|---|---|
| Late Change Visibility — Late design changes disrupt supplier coordination | Early Sourcing Visibility — Early design assessment of supplier feasibility pre-RFQ |
| Inconsistent Quote Scope — Inconsistent proposal scopes hinder quote comparison | Unified Sourcing Data — Unified view of specs, target costs, supplier data |
| Manual Quote Consolidation — Manual quote analysis creates heavy workloads | Automated Cost Roll-up — Auto-comparison of supplier cost structures/quotes |
| Unclear Decision Basis — Hard to justify supplier selection transparently | Supplier Comparison Support — Data-driven optimal supplier selection and negotiation |

---

## Quality Manager
> "I need early visibility to catch supplier process risks before they turn into production defects."

### Goal
Ensure supplier process readiness to prevent quality defects before production starts.

### KPIs
- Supplier Defect Rate
- PPAP First-pass Rate
- On-time Quality Completion
- Early Risk Detection Rate

### Pain Points & Needs

| Pain Point | Need |
|---|---|
| Late Process Visibility — Late supplier process readiness assessment | Early Manufacturing Visibility — Early process/control plan validation from design |
| Inconsistent APQP Maturity — APQP execution gaps complicate management | Structured APQP Governance — Standardized quality plan and milestone tracking system |
| Fragmented Quality Lessons — Fragmented lessons learned from past defects | Data-driven Defect Prevention — Past defect linkage prevents recurrence |
| Reactive Issue Management — Reactive quality management after defects occur | Unclear Supplier Feasibility — Proactive pre-mass production monitoring |

---

## Supplier (External Portal User)
> "I need clear change visibility and RFQ status to quote accurately and collaborate without rework."

### Goal
Deliver accurate quotes the first time by accessing transparent BOM and RFQ data.

### KPIs
- Quote-to-Order Cycle Time
- RFQ Win Rate
- First-pass Quote Accuracy
- Requote Rate

### Pain Points & Needs

| Pain Point | Need |
|---|---|
| Ambiguous BOM Changes — Unclear BOM/hidden changes cause quote errors | Version-controlled BOM Data — Systematic BOM with design change history and real-time alerts |
| Fragmented Communication — Fragmented files/manual comms hurt efficiency | Standardized RFQ Collaboration — Single portal for quote submission and document exchange |
| Unknown RFQ Status — Lack of progress/priority visibility hinders internal response | Transparent RFQ Tracking — Real-time visibility into sourcing decisions and milestones |
| Late-stage Engagement — Late post-freeze involvement limits manufacturing feedback | Early Design Collaboration — Early design channel for manufacturing expertise |
